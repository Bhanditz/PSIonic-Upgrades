Flash Ring
  A portable mini-programmer that can cast spells at 4x their regular Psi cost.
  It's in the shape of a ring, missing slots from the center and outside of the programmer.
  It can be used as a regular spell bullet, loaded into a programmer, or snapped into a magazine.

Flow Plated Exosuit
  An exosuit that is plated with either Ivory or Ebony.
  It has no special properties armor-wise or Psi-wise from the Exosuit.
  It has lines that glow with the user's colorizer.
